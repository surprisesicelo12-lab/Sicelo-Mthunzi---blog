About Me

Hello! I'm Sicelo Mthunzi, a passionate rugby player from Johannesburg, South Africa. I started playing rugby in Grade 8 at Highlands North Boys High School and quickly joined the Lions Rugby Union development sides. Over the years, I have captained my school's first team, leading by example while fostering a supportive environment for my teammates. I pride myself on dominant carries, creative lineouts, and accurate throws, always emphasizing teamwork so my teammates can shine on the field.

I also play for Wasps Rugby Club, where I was named Rising Star of the Year in my first season. Inspired by Siphoseethu Mnebelele, former SA Schools captain, I aspire to play professional rugby after school.
